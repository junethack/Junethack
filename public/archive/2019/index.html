Welcome to Junethack!

Are you ready to embark on a perilous journey through the Dungeons of Doom? Ready to collect all the treasures their chthonic depths have to offer? Ready to kill all your enemies and EAT their corpses? Ready to challenge the mightiest demons of Gehennom and battle the mighty Wizard of Yendor? And BADASS enough to travel the Heavens themselves while kicking angels in the face?

Then play NetHack.

And also play in Junethack, the NetHack Cross-Variant Summer Tournament (mostly) held in June.

You can view the results of the Junethack 2018, Junethack 2017, Junethack 2016, Junethack 2015, Junethack 2014, Junethack 2013, Junethack 2012, Junethack 2011 tournaments.

And the tournament rules at the Rules page.

Or stare longingly at the competitions and trophies up for grabs at the Trophies page.

Games played on the servers listed on the Servers page are eligible for the tournament.
